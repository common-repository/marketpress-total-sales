== Description ==


You may want to show off your site's total sales  on the front-end of your site. This plugin adds a new shortcode
 `[MPTS]`, that will do just that! it also supports multisite.


**Example Use:**


 Show off your site's total sales

 You want to brag to your customers how much money you're making

 You want to brag to your customers how much money you're making
 from each site in case of multisite. 

The Shortcode: `[MPTS]`



== Installation ==

1. Either download the plugin and upload it or search for it under Plugins > Add New. Activate it, too.
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Use the [MPTS] shortcode in post or pages 
4. Place `<?php do_shortcode('[MPTS]'); ?>` in your templates
5. Be awesome!
